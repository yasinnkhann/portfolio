Welcome to Sleaks-Slinks, an E-commerce site for fashion and accessories! Here, all products are fetched from an API and the most popular ones are displayed in the overview section. Related products that the consumer may be interested in are shown in a carousel fashion and can even be added to their outfits. People can post questions and answers about a given product as well as ratings and reviews.
