Have you ever had a tough time deciding what movie or series to watch? Or maybe you wish there was something you could use to track everything you were currently watching, have watched, or plan to watch and even give ratings to each of them? I present to you, Animedia! An app perfect for movies and shows enthusiasts. You can even discover actors/actresses and find shows and movies they're known for!
